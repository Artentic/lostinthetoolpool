## 1. Major Retailer Programs

### 1.1 Amazon Associates

Amazon is the largest general affiliate program and the default starting point for most affiliate sites. However, commission rates for tools are not impressive.

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Commission Rate** | **3%** on Tools & Home Improvement |
| **Cookie Duration** | **24 hours** (extends to **90 days** if item is added to cart) |
| **Payment Threshold** | $10 (direct deposit), $100 (check) |
| **Payment Schedule** | ~60 days after end of earnings month |
| **Payment Methods** | Direct deposit (international bank transfer to 52 countries including Belgium), check ($15 fee), Amazon gift card |
| **Platform** | In-house (Amazon Associates Central) |
| **Product Restrictions** | Gift cards, wireless plans, alcohol, Kindle subscriptions, vehicles, pet medications earn 0% |
| **Application Requirements** | Website or mobile app required; must generate 3 qualifying sales within first 180 days or account is closed |
| **International Notes** | Non-US affiliates accepted. Separate accounts needed per country (US, UK, DE, FR, etc.). International Direct Deposit available to Belgium (EUR). |

**Full Commission Rate Table (2026):**

| Category | Rate |
|---|---|
| Luxury Beauty, Amazon Explore | 10% |
| Digital Music, Physical Music, Handmade, Digital Videos | 5% |
| Physical Books, Kitchen, Automotive | 4.5% |
| Fashion, Apparel, Echo, Ring, Watches, Jewelry, Luggage, Shoes | 4% |
| **Tools, Home Improvement**, Furniture, Home, Lawn & Garden, Pets, Sports, Toys, Baby, Outdoors, Musical Instruments | **3%** |
| PC, PC Components, DVD & Blu-Ray | 2.5% |
| Televisions, Digital Video Games | 2% |
| Amazon Fresh, Groceries, Health & Personal Care, Physical Video Games | 1% |
| All Other Categories | 4% |

**Pros:**
- Massive product catalog — virtually every tool from every brand
- High consumer trust and conversion rates
- 90-day cart cookie is valuable (users often add tools to cart and buy later)
- International Direct Deposit supports EUR to Belgian banks
- Universal link — if user buys ANYTHING within 24 hours, you earn commission on it all

**Cons:**
- 3% is low for tools (a $200 drill earns $6)
- 24-hour base cookie is very short
- Commission rates have been cut repeatedly over the years (tools were 6-8% pre-2020)
- Amazon can change rates at any time with minimal notice
- Cannot use affiliate links in email

**Special Notes:**
- The "add to cart" cookie extension to 90 days is a major advantage for high-consideration purchases like power tools. Users often research, add to cart, then buy days later.
- Amazon's "bounty" programs pay flat fees for certain sign-ups (Prime trials, Audible, etc.) which can supplement tool commissions.

## 7. Tax Implications for CYBERNITED (Belgium)

### 7.1 US-Belgium Tax Treaty

Belgium and the United States have an income tax treaty (signed November 27, 2006) that significantly reduces or eliminates withholding tax on cross-border income.

**Key treaty provisions relevant to affiliate income:**

| Income Type | Default US Withholding | Treaty Rate |
|---|---|---|
| Royalties | 30% | **0%** |
| Interest | 30% | **0%** (for most types) |
| Business profits (no PE) | 30% | **0%** (not taxable in US if no Permanent Establishment) |
| Dividends (portfolio) | 30% | 15% |
| Dividends (direct, 10%+ ownership) | 30% | 5% |

**Critical question: How are affiliate commissions classified?**

Affiliate commissions are generally classified as **business profits** under US tax treaties, NOT as royalties. Under Article 7 of the US-Belgium treaty, business profits of a Belgian enterprise are taxable ONLY in Belgium unless the enterprise has a Permanent Establishment (PE) in the United States.

**CYBERNITED does NOT have a PE in the US** (no office, no employees, no fixed place of business), so affiliate commissions should be **exempt from US withholding tax** under the treaty, provided the W-8BEN-E form is properly filed claiming treaty benefits.

### 7.2 W-8BEN-E Form Requirements

Since CYBERNITED is a Belgian company (not an individual), it must file **Form W-8BEN-E** (not W-8BEN, which is for individuals).

**Key filing requirements:**

- **Part I:** Identification of Beneficial Owner — CYBERNITED company details, Belgian address, Belgian enterprise number (as foreign TIN)
- **Part II:** Claim of Tax Treaty Benefits — Select Belgium as country of residence. Cite Article 7 (Business Profits) of the US-Belgium treaty. Claim 0% withholding rate.
- **Part III:** Entity type classification — Likely "Corporation" or appropriate Belgian entity type
- **Validity:** Form is valid for 3 calendar years from signing, then must be renewed
- **Each platform requires its own form:** Amazon, Impact, Rakuten, CJ, Awin, etc. will each require a separate W-8BEN-E submission

**Without a valid W-8BEN-E, US platforms will withhold 30% of all commission payments.** This is the single most important administrative task.

### 7.3 Belgian Tax Obligations

Affiliate income earned by CYBERNITED from US sources is taxable in Belgium under normal Belgian corporate tax rules:

- Belgian corporate tax rate: 25% (20% on first EUR 100,000 for qualifying SMEs)
- US affiliate income must be declared as foreign-source business income
- No double taxation — if any US tax is withheld despite treaty claims, Belgium provides a foreign tax credit
- VAT: Affiliate commissions are B2B services. Under reverse charge mechanism, no VAT is charged by US platforms, and CYBERNITED may need to self-assess Belgian VAT (21%) on the service received, with simultaneous deduction if used for taxable business activities (net zero effect).

### 7.4 Practical Steps

1. **Immediately upon approval for each affiliate program:** Submit W-8BEN-E claiming treaty benefits under Article 7 (Business Profits) with 0% withholding rate
2. **Maintain records** of all affiliate income by platform and pay period
3. **Consult a Belgian tax advisor** familiar with US-source income to confirm the classification of affiliate commissions and proper reporting
4. **Renew W-8BEN-E forms** before they expire (every 3 years)
5. **Track currency conversions** — affiliate income will be in USD and must be converted to EUR for Belgian tax reporting at the applicable exchange rate

## 8. Payment Solutions for EU-Based Affiliates

Amazon offers direct international bank transfers to Belgian banks in EUR at no charge from Amazon's side. This is the simplest option for Amazon commissions. Your bank may charge a small incoming international transfer fee.

- Minimum payout: $10 equivalent
- No Amazon fees
- Converted to EUR at Amazon's exchange rate

## 9. FTC Disclosure Requirements

Even though CYBERNITED is a Belgian company, because the site targets US consumers and earns commissions from US affiliate programs, **FTC disclosure rules apply**.

### 9.1 Core Requirements

The Federal Trade Commission requires that any **material connection** between an affiliate and a brand/retailer be **clearly and conspicuously** disclosed to consumers.

### 9.2 What "Clear and Conspicuous" Means

- Disclosure must be **near the affiliate link**, not buried in a footer or separate page
- Must be **visible before the user clicks** the link
- Must be in **plain language** that the average consumer understands
- Must be **large enough to read** on all devices (mobile first)
- Cannot be hidden behind a click ("click here for disclosure")
- Hashtags like #ad or #sponsored are NOT sufficient on their own

### 9.3 Required Disclosure Language

**On every page with affiliate links, include a clear statement such as:**

> "We earn commissions from purchases made through links on this site. This doesn't affect our recommendations — we recommend tools based on quality and value, not commission rates. [Full disclosure](/disclosure)"

**Near individual affiliate links (or in a prominent page banner):**

> "As an affiliate, we earn from qualifying purchases."

### 9.4 Recommended Implementation

1. **Global site banner/header:** A persistent, visible disclosure on every page stating the site earns affiliate commissions
2. **Dedicated disclosure page** (`/disclosure` or `/affiliate-disclosure`): Full details about which affiliate programs the site participates in, how commissions work, and that recommendations are independent
3. **Near affiliate links:** Visual indicator (e.g., small shopping bag icon or "affiliate link" text) on or near each outbound product link
4. **In content:** First mention of a product with an affiliate link should include or be preceded by a disclosure

### 9.5 Penalties for Non-Compliance (2026)

- Civil penalties up to **$51,744 per violation**
- Each post/page with undisclosed affiliate links counts as a separate violation
- FTC increased enforcement against smaller creators in 2025-2026
- A site with 10 non-disclosed pages could face fines exceeding **$500,000**

### 9.6 Additional Considerations for Non-US Companies

- FTC jurisdiction extends to any entity that affects US commerce, regardless of where the company is based
- Being based in Belgium does NOT exempt CYBERNITED from FTC rules
- EU transparency requirements (especially under the Digital Services Act) impose similar or stricter disclosure obligations for EU audiences
- Implement disclosures that satisfy BOTH FTC and EU requirements
