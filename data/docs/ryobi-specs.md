## Platform Overview

Ryobi (owned by TTI Group, same parent as Milwaukee and Ridgid) operates three distinct battery platforms:

| Platform | Voltage | Target | Tool Count | Exclusive Retailer |
|----------|---------|--------|------------|-------------------|
| 18V ONE+ | 18V | DIY homeowners | 492+ products | Home Depot |
| 40V | 40V | Outdoor power | 141+ products | Home Depot |
| USB Lithium | 4V | Compact/portable | 43+ products | Home Depot |

**Key differentiator**: Ryobi ONE+ is the largest single-battery platform in the world with 492+ tools sharing one battery. No other brand comes close to this breadth of tool selection for DIY users.

**Cross-platform compatibility**: The 18V ONE+ and 40V platforms are NOT cross-compatible. Different battery shapes, electrical contacts, and voltage ratings make them physically and electrically incompatible. There are no official adapters. Ryobi does offer a dual-platform charger (model for charging both 18V and 40V batteries on one unit).

## 18V ONE+ Battery System

### Battery Backward Compatibility

Ryobi's biggest selling point: **Every 18V ONE+ battery works with every ONE+ tool ever made.** The platform launched in 1996 (30 years ago) and has maintained backward and forward compatibility throughout. A battery purchased today will work with a tool from 2000, and vice versa.

### Battery Tiers

Ryobi offers three tiers of 18V batteries:

#### Standard ONE+ Batteries
Basic lithium-ion cells, LED fuel gauge, IntelliCell technology for power management.

| Model | Capacity | Type | Key Features |
|-------|----------|------|-------------|
| PBP002 | 1.5Ah | Standard | Entry-level, lightweight, compact |
| PBP006C | 2.0Ah | Standard | Compact design, 30-60 min recharge |
| PBP005C | 4.0Ah | Standard | Most popular model, 2x energy vs 2.0Ah |
| P193 | 6.0Ah | Standard | 108Wh capacity, good for garden tools |
| P194 | 9.0Ah | Standard | Largest standard, 162Wh, 2.25-4.5 hr recharge |

#### HP (High Performance) Batteries
Advanced lithium cells with additional 2 contact terminals for communication with HP brushless tools. Provide up to 6x more runtime, 30% more power, run cooler.

| Model | Capacity | Type | Key Features |
|-------|----------|------|-------------|
| PBP003 | 2.0Ah | HP | Cool-running cells, optimized for high drain |
| PBP004 | 4.0Ah | HP | INTELLICELL + COOL-CORE technology |
| PBP007 | 6.0Ah | HP | Optimized for high-drain applications |

#### EDGE Batteries (Newest Technology)
Uses larger 21700 lithium-ion cells (vs 18650 in standard/HP). Tabless cells for more efficient energy transfer. Cool-Core Pro heat management with improved heat sinks and cell straps.

| Model | Capacity | Type | Key Features |
|-------|----------|------|-------------|
| (varies) | 4.0Ah | EDGE | Up to 2x power and 4x runtime vs HP |
| (varies) | 8.0Ah | EDGE | 21700 cells, lower internal resistance |
| (varies) | 12.0Ah | EDGE | Maximum capacity, 21700 cells |

### Key Battery Differences

| Feature | Standard | HP | EDGE |
|---------|----------|----|----|
| Cell type | 18650 | 18650 | 21700 |
| Extra contacts | No | Yes (2 extra) | Yes |
| Communicates with HP tools | No | Yes | Yes |
| Cool-Core technology | No | Yes | Yes (Pro) |
| Tabless cells | No | No | Yes |
| Works with ALL ONE+ tools | Yes | Yes | Yes |

**Important**: ALL batteries work with ALL ONE+ tools. HP and EDGE batteries just deliver extra performance when paired with HP brushless tools that have matching contacts.

## EDGE Battery Technology

### What Makes EDGE Different

EDGE represents Ryobi's newest and most advanced battery technology, available for both 18V and 40V platforms.

| Feature | Standard | HP | EDGE |
|---------|----------|----|----|
| Cell size | 18650 | 18650 | **21700** (larger) |
| Cell design | Standard | Standard | **Tabless** (more efficient) |
| Heat management | Basic | Cool-Core | **Cool-Core Pro** |
| Power vs HP | Baseline | 1x | **Up to 2x** |
| Runtime vs HP | Baseline | 1x | **Up to 4x** |
| Temperature | Standard | Cooler | **25% cooler than HP** |
| Backward compatible | Yes | Yes | **Yes** |

### Why EDGE Matters
- 21700 cells store more energy in similar physical size
- Tabless design reduces internal resistance = more efficient power delivery
- Cool-Core Pro prevents overheating in demanding applications
- Enables cordless tools to compete with corded/gas equivalents on runtime
