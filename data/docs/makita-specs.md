## Battery Systems Overview

Makita operates **four distinct battery platforms**. These systems are **NOT cross-compatible** with each other (LXT batteries do not fit XGT tools and vice versa), though Makita offers adapter solutions in some cases.

| System | Voltage | Tool Count | Target |
|--------|---------|-----------|--------|
| 18V LXT | 18V (36V with X2 dual-battery) | 350+ products | Professional / DIY |
| 40V max XGT | 40V (80V with X2 dual-battery) | 170+ products | Heavy-duty professional |
| 12V max CXT | 12V | 30+ products | Compact / precision tasks |
| ConnectX | 36V (backpack power) | Limited lineup | Commercial OPE |

## Proprietary Technologies

### Star Protection

- **What it is:** Real-time digital communication between battery and tool
- **How it works:** Battery and tool exchange data on temperature, voltage, and current during use
- **Protection against:** Overloading, over-discharging, and overheating
- **Compatibility indicator:** Look for a star symbol or yellow connector plate on the tool's battery receiver
- **Available on:** LXT and XGT platforms
- **Benefit:** Extends battery and tool life, prevents damage from extreme conditions

### AVT (Anti-Vibration Technology)

- **What it is:** A synchronized, three-component vibration reduction system
- **Components:**
  1. **Air-actuated counterbalance:** Uses air pressure to produce counterweight motion, reducing vibration
  2. **Vibration-absorbing housing:** Moves independently of motor and hammer mechanism, isolating vibration
  3. **Integrated damper spring:** At the base of the impact bolt, absorbs impact vibrations from the drill bit
- **Engineering basis:** Based on seismic engineering principles (same damping science used to protect buildings from earthquakes)
- **Result:** Reduces vibration to as low as 5 m/s2
- **Found on:** Rotary hammers, reciprocating saws, and demolition hammers (both LXT and XGT)

### AWS (Auto-Start Wireless System)

- **What it is:** Bluetooth-based wireless communication between tool and dust extractor
- **How it works:**
  1. Bluetooth chip in both the tool and the dust extractor
  2. Pair the devices once
  3. When tool trigger is pulled, dust extractor starts automatically
  4. When tool stops, dust extractor stops
- **Capacity:** Up to 10 AWS tools can be linked to one AWS dust extractor
- **Compatible tool categories:** Circular saws, miter saws, rotary hammers, routers, planers, sanders, grinders (select models marked "AWS Capable")
- **Compatible dust extractors:** Select Makita dust extractors with AWS receiver built in
- **Benefit:** Eliminates the need for a power cord connection between tool and extractor; enables cordless dust collection

### AFT (Active Feedback-Sensing Technology)

- **What it is:** Electronic safety system for rotary tools
- **How it works:** If the bit/blade rotation is suddenly forced to stop (bind-up), the motor electronically shuts off
- **Benefit:** Prevents dangerous kickback and runaway rotation
- **Found on:** Rotary hammers, grinders, circular saws (both LXT and XGT)

### ADT (Active Torque Drive)

- **What it is:** Auto-speed technology that adjusts torque and speed based on load
- **Benefit:** Optimal performance without user intervention
