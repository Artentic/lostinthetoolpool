### Paslode (7 products)

**Platform**: 7V Li-ion battery + fuel cell (proprietary hybrid system)
**Sales Model**: Home Depot, Lowes, specialty fastener distributors
**Price Range**: $299 (brad nailer) to $590 (siding nailer)
**Target**: Framing crews, trim carpenters, siding installers

**Product Categories Covered**:
- Framing Nailers: CFN325XP (cordless), CF325XPRO24 (cordless extended), PowerMaster Plus (pneumatic)
- Finish Nailers: 916200 (cordless angled 16ga), 902400 (cordless straight 16ga)
- Brad Nailers: 918100 (cordless 18ga)
- Siding Nailers: 919100 CSF (cordless)

**Key Differentiators**:
- Pioneered cordless nailing (fuel cell + battery technology)
- No compressor needed -- true jobsite freedom
- 7V Li-ion battery delivers 9,000-12,000 nails per charge
- Fuel cell provides combustion power for driving nails
- Works in cold temperatures (down to 14 deg F)
- Lightest cordless finish nailer on market (4.6 lbs)

**Fuel Cell Technology Explained**:
Unlike battery-only cordless nailers (Milwaukee, DeWalt), Paslode uses a combustion system. A fuel cell contains pressurized gas that ignites in a small combustion chamber to drive the piston. The battery powers ignition and the fan that clears exhaust. This gives Paslode nailers a distinctive "pop" sound and requires periodic fuel cell replacement (~1,200 nails per framing fuel cell).

## Ecosystem Compatibility Notes

- **Hilti Nuron**: Closed ecosystem. All 22V batteries work across all Nuron tools. Not compatible with any other brand.
- **Festool 18V**: Closed ecosystem. All 18V batteries work across all Festool cordless tools. New Tabless batteries (2026) are backward compatible.
- **Dremel 12V**: The 12V batteries work across 8240 and 8250 models. The 8V (8150) and 4V (7760) use separate batteries. Not cross-compatible with Bosch 12V despite same parent company.
- **Klein Tools**: 20V battery platform is specific to Klein battery-operated tools. Not cross-compatible with other brands.
- **Knipex**: Hand tools only. No batteries or power platform.
- **Paslode**: Proprietary 7V Li-ion battery. All current cordless nailers share the same battery. Fuel cells are nailer-type specific (framing vs. trim/finish).
- **Bostitch**: Pneumatic tools need any compatible air compressor (brand-agnostic). 20V MAX cordless tools use Bostitch-specific batteries (technically compatible with some DeWalt 20V MAX packs but not officially supported).
