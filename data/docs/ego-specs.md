### Core Principle

### Battery Technology Features

- **ARC Lithium:** Advanced Rechargeable Cell technology -- EGO's proprietary battery architecture
- **56V nominal voltage:** Higher voltage = more power per amp-hour vs 18V/20V systems
- **Shock-resistant design:** Drop and weather protection
- **Integrated fuel gauge:** LED indicators displaying remaining charge
- **Patented power management:** Optimized performance and battery life
- **Phase-change material thermal management:** (2.5Ah - 5.0Ah models) passive cooling
- **Universal compatibility:** Every battery works with every EGO tool and charger
- **3-year limited warranty** on all batteries

### Peak Power Technology

- **What it is:** Combines the power of two EGO 56V batteries simultaneously in dual-port tools
- **Used in:** Snow blowers, riding mowers, pressure washers, select high-demand tools
- **Benefit:** Effectively doubles available power without changing voltage platform

### POWERLOAD Technology
- Feed the line into the head, press a button, and the trimmer automatically winds the line
- Eliminates manual line winding entirely
- Available on most current-generation trimmers

### LINE IQ Technology
- Automatically feeds and cuts line to optimal length
- Eliminates bump feeding
- Available on ST1623T and select newer models

| Backpack Blower | ~70 dB | 80+ dB | 8x quieter (EGO claim) |

### Emissions

| Metric | EGO | Gas |

| Direct emissions | Zero | Significant CO, HC, NOx |
